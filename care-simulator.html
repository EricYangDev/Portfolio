Case Study 01 • EdTech + Health Education

Care Simulator + Scenario-to-Quiz Builder

A compact learning experience that demonstrates branching dialogue, learner feedback, editable activity generation, and health-education content design.

Try Simulator Try Quiz Builder
Design goal

Practice before performance

Help learners make low-risk choices, receive immediate feedback, and reflect on communication quality before real-world use.

Problem

Static lessons do not always create confident decisions.

Learners can understand a concept but still struggle when they need to respond to a person, choose the right tone, or translate content into practice. This demo turns content into scenario choices and feedback loops.

Learning objective Practice supportive, clear, psychologically safe communication.
Technical objective Show client-side JavaScript logic, editable content generation, and reusable UI patterns.
Evaluation objective Measure choices, completion, and feedback quality locally in the browser.

Why this works as a portfolio demo
